Meet the Chef!

Manera Jackson

Chef Manera Jackson, A Texas native who found her profound passion for cooking early in life, ignited by her home economics teacher and nurtured by her grandmother and aunts.

Despite an initial detour from culinary school, she eventually honed her skills at Le Cordon Bleu, leading her to establish MaNae's Cuisine, specializing in healthy, Southern-inspired dishes which she later expanded to catering and teaching.

Her diverse culinary talents also extend to baking, with her Caramel Macchiato Cake being a best-seller, all while she finds deep connections between her love for music and the art of cooking, believing that food, like music, is truly food for the soul.

WEEKLY MEAL DELIVERY

MaNae's Cuisine offers convenient weekly meal delivery services, bringing healthy, southern-inspired dishes right to your doorstep. Our meal prep service is designed for busy professionals, families, and individuals who want to enjoy delicious, home-cooked meals without the hassle of daily cooking.

Each meal is prepared with quality ingredients, slow-cooking to allow the flavors to blend, creating a rich and satisfying profile that will delight the taste buds.
